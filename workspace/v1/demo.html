Garfield, AZ!

The High School Media Innovation Camp at the Cronkite School offered future journalists, game developers and other students interested in media and technology the chance to experiment with cutting-edge tools, including augmented and virtual reality technologies, news games and apps. Students used this opportunity to explore "Revitalization vs Gentrification" through the story of the Garfield, AZ neighborhood.
